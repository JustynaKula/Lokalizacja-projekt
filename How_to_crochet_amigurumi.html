# How to crochet amigurumi **Amigurumi** is the Japanese art of knitting or crocheting small, stuffed yarn creatures. What you need: - [Yarn](#yarn) - [Types and colors](#types-and-colors) - [Types](#types) - [Colors](#colors) - [Fiberfill](#fiberfill) - [Tools](#tools) - [Hook](#hook) - [Yarn needle](#yarn-needle) - [Stitch markers](#stitch-markers) Crocheting amigurumi can be a lot of fun. You just need to try it yourself. **Ready?** **Steady?** __Crochet away!__ It is very _easy_. *Not* difficult as some think.
